Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ReadSage

What file formats does ReadSage support?

ReadSage supports PDF, EPUB, and TXT formats. You can upload textbooks, novels, technical manuals, academic papers, or any learning material in these formats.

How does ReadSage generate questions?

Our AI analyzes your book's content, identifying key concepts, facts, and relationships. It then generates diverse question types (MCQ, code, equations, essays) tailored to the material's complexity and subject matter.

Can I choose specific chapters or topics?

Yes! ReadSage automatically divides your book into chapters and topics. You can select specific sections to create targeted quizzes, perfect for exam prep or reviewing difficult material.

What types of questions can ReadSage create?

ReadSage generates 10+ question types including Multiple Choice (MCQ), Single Choice, Text Answers, Mathematical Equations, Code Challenges, Essay Questions, and more - adapted to your book's subject.

Is my book data private and secure?

Absolutely! All your books and data are encrypted and stored securely on our servers. We respect your privacy and never share your data with third parties. Your reading progress and quiz results are private to you only. ReadSage is fully GDPR compliant.

How does progress tracking work?

ReadSage tracks your chapter completion percentage, accuracy scores for each quiz, daily learning streaks, and question difficulty levels. You can visualize your improvement over time with detailed analytics dashboards.

Can I use ReadSage for exam preparation?

Yes! ReadSage is perfect for exam prep. Upload your study materials, select relevant chapters, and generate practice tests. Get instant feedback with detailed explanations to identify and strengthen weak areas before your exam.

Does ReadSage work with textbooks?

Absolutely! ReadSage excels with textbooks across all subjects - mathematics, chemistry, history, programming, literature, physics, biology, business, and more. It adapts question types to match the subject matter.
